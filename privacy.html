Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Airtable Lead Enricher extension ("Extension", "we", "our") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

The Extension operates entirely within your Airtable workspace and does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. Specifically:

Data You Provide:

  • Airtable Data: The Extension reads company information from your selected Airtable records (company name, website, location) to perform enrichment.
  • API Keys: You provide API keys (Apify, OpenAI, Hunter.io) which are stored locally in your browser using Airtable's GlobalConfig API. These keys never leave your browser except when making API calls to the respective services.
  • Configuration Settings: Field mappings, filter preferences, and other settings are stored locally in your browser.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the enrichment functionality:

  • Enrichment Processing: Company data from your Airtable is sent to Apify actors to retrieve enriched business information (email, phone, social profiles, etc.)
  • AI Analysis: If you configure OpenAI integration, company data is sent to OpenAI's API for lead scoring and ICP matching
  • Social Profile Lookup: If you configure Hunter.io, domain information is sent to Hunter.io to find social media profiles
  • Results Storage: Enriched data is written back to your Airtable base. No copies are retained by us.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All configuration settings and API keys are stored locally in your browser using Airtable's secure GlobalConfig mechanism
  • No Server Storage: We do not operate any servers that store your data
  • Airtable Security: Your data remains within Airtable's infrastructure, protected by their security measures
  • Third-Party Services: When you use third-party APIs (Apify, OpenAI, Hunter.io), data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to those services and subject to their respective privacy policies

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal information. Data is only shared with third-party services that you explicitly configure:

  • Apify: Company data is sent to Apify actors for enrichment processing
  • OpenAI: Company data is sent for AI-powered lead scoring (only if you provide an API key)
  • Hunter.io: Domain information is sent for social profile lookup (only if you provide an API key)

Third-Party Services

This Extension integrates with the following third-party services. Please review their privacy policies:

  • Airtable: https://www.airtable.com/privacy
  • Apify: https://apify.com/privacy-policy
  • OpenAI: https://openai.com/privacy
  • Hunter.io: https://hunter.io/privacy-policy

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: All your data remains in your Airtable base, fully accessible to you
  • Data Deletion: You can delete enriched data from your Airtable at any time
  • API Key Management: You can remove stored API keys by clearing your Airtable Extension settings
  • Service Opt-Out: You can choose not to use optional integrations (OpenAI, Hunter.io) at any time

Children's Privacy

This Extension is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when changes were last made. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: Report an issue
  • Documentation: Extension Documentation

Compliance

This Extension is designed to comply with data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA. Since all data processing occurs within your Airtable workspace and your chosen third-party services, you maintain control over your data at all times.
